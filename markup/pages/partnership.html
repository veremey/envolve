Partnership Program (Research)

As the largest provider of Medicaid and Health Insurance Marketplace services in the United States Centene at its core had a community driven focus. The Envolve Center will expand its reach by connecting each of Centene’s Healthplans with research partners from local academic institutions, community-based organizations, and care providers.

Why Partner with Us?

Committed to innovation in whole person health? Interested in partnering towards the common goal of designing healthcare interventions that translate to the enhanced delivery of managed care? Becoming an Envolve Center Partner offers unique opportunities and benefits:

  • With over 12 million members, Centene possess a vast amount of analytic information that can be used to enhance research and inform studies

  • Targeted research that is designed to be applied ensures that your findings will not “sit on a shelf” - we implement our findings and share our knowledge

  • Opportunity to publish

  • Build evidence in support of grants and other academic funding opportunities

Research Focus Areas

Using the most rigorous and established public health and behavioral science evidence, the Envolve Center develops and tests innovative research focused on health outcomes. There are five areas of focus. For more information, click here.
