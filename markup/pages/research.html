Our Research Goals

Using the most rigorous and established public health and behavioral science evidence, the Envolve Center develops and tests innovative interventions and strategies, applying them in real-world populations and generating new understanding of best practices for implementation and improved health outcomes.

Under the umbrella of the Envolve Center’s mission to improve the health of vulnerable populations through behavior-based research interventions, our research goals include:

  • Improve the effectiveness and reach of health programs and services, including vehicles such as online platforms, telephonic and home health coaching, treatment plans, and other efforts to better empower consumers to manage chronic illnesses and live more healthfully.

  • Contribute to the scientific evidence about what works in changing health behavior, improving health outcomes, and facilitating fuller lives for consumers.

  • Serve as a model organization for translational research, training, implementation and dissemination.

Research Focus Areas

The Envolve Center has five areas of research focus including engagement, health outcomes, behaviors, social determinants of health, and healthcare service delivery.

Within these areas of research, our studies address a number of critical health topics affecting thousands of individuals and families from our local communities, cities and states.

Research topics include pediatric obesity, diabetes, substance abuse, smoking cessation, behavioral/mental health, preventive health and wellness, the role of basic needs and life values in health behaviors and outcomes, and more.

To learn more about the studies in each of our five focus areas, click on the area of interest below.:

Member Engagement

​​Develop and test the most effective strategies for communicating and encouraging individuals and their families to participate in health related programs.

Behaviors

​Study ways to support individuals in making healthier choices, identify strategies for overcoming barriers that impede healthy choices and sustained behavior change.

Health Outcomes

​Focus on improving health outcomes for specific conditions such as diabetes, asthma, depression, substance use disorder, and populations such as pregnant women and children, elderly and the vulnerable.

Healthcare Service Delivery

​Investigate the impact of service delivery models on health outcomes and assess the role these models play in people’s decisions and Healthcare utilization behaviors.

Social Determinants of Health

​Explore how various social and economic factors such as the lack of housing, food, and safety affect the health and well-being of individuals.
