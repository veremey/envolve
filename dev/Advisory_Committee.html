Vision

By design, the Center is distinctive in structure and propelled by a lifecycle that is an informed collaboration. Our researchers are experts and our work is in real world settings with a focus on issues that allow individuals to lead healthy, productive lives. We accelerate solutions by collapsing the supply chain, giving us intentional work with intentional results.

MISSION STATEMENT

The Center is a Community-Industry-Academic Healthcare Partnership that advances life-centric health solutions to improve lives so that communities can thrive.
