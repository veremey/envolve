April 09, 2018 share
Eat Your Way to a Brighter, Whiter Smile! Dan Ariely, Envolve Center faculty director and James B. Duke Professor of Psychology and Behavioral Economics at Duke University, presented his research findings from a comprehensive survey conducted with 1,000 adults with type 2 diabetes. Dan discussed the factors that drive decision making and their impact on diabetes self-care behaviors and health outcomes. The 45-minute presentation was followed by a 15-minute Q&A.
